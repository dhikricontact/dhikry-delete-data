Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-08

Tasbih Counter is a dhikr and tasbih application that offers manual counting, voice counting, daily wird planning, local reminders, and local history views.

Data We Store

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Current tasbih count and count history
  • Selected dhikr and daily wird configuration
  • Daily reminder settings
  • Weekly daily wird completion history
  • Last app open time used for inactivity reminders

Microphone And Speech Recognition

If you choose voice counting, the app requests microphone and speech recognition access. These permissions are used only to listen for dhikr phrases and update the counter live.

Speech recognition may be handled by the speech recognition service provided by your device operating system or platform provider. This behavior depends on the platform and system configuration.

Notifications

The app can send local notifications for:

  • Daily wird reminders at a time chosen by the user
  • Inactivity reminders after more than 24 hours without opening the app
  • Completion messages when the daily wird is finished

Data Sharing

Tasbih Counter does not create user accounts and does not intentionally transmit your dhikr history, daily wird plan, or reminder settings to our own servers.

The app does not sell personal data.

Your Choices

  • Use the manual counter without voice input
  • Disable daily reminders
  • Reset counts
  • Clear the daily wird
  • Revoke permissions through your device settings
