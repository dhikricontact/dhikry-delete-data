Delete Your Data

Dhikri

Dhikri stores tasbih counts, daily wird settings, reminder settings, and local history on your device.

How to delete your data in the app

  1. Open the Dhikri app.
  2. Open Privacy Policy inside the app.
  3. Tap Delete My Data.
  4. Confirm deletion.

This deletes all locally stored app data on the device, including:

  • Current counters
  • Dhikr history
  • Daily wird configuration
  • Weekly completion history
  • Reminder settings
